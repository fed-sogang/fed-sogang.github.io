My research focuses on designing human-centered interactions with AI to foster more inclusive and meaningful experiences with technology.

I lead the Future Experience Design Group, where we explore how creative design can bridge the gap between emerging technologies and society. Our work brings together UX/UI methodologies and human-centered design to develop socially impactful and ethically responsible solutions.

I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Industrial Design from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), where I was advised by Prof. Sangsu Lee in the Next Interface Lab. I completed my undergraduate studies at Hongik University, earning a BFA in Sculpture and Art Education with a second major in Metal Arts and Design.
